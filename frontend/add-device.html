🚀 Setup Your Device

Follow these steps to connect your device:

1️⃣ Flash the firmware

Upload the Open IoT firmware to your ESP from the esp_firmware/ folder.

2️⃣ Connect to WiFi

Your device creates a WiFi AP named OpenIoT-Setup. Connect and enter your WiFi credentials + device params.

3️⃣ Adopt the device

The device will automatically call home with the adoption token. Once verified, it appears on your dashboard.

4️⃣ ✅ Done!

Your device starts publishing sensor data via MQTT. Check your dashboard for real-time updates.
